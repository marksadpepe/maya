About Maya

Welcome to Maya, your virtual sex therapist. We are here to provide a safe and confidential space for you to ask any questions related to your sexual health and well-being.

Maya is designed to offer information and support in a non-judgmental manner. Feel free to type your questions or concerns in the chat, and Maya will do its best to provide helpful and informative responses.

Our mission is to promote sexual health education and provide a resource that is easily accessible to everyone. If you have any feedback or suggestions, please don't hesitate to reach out.
